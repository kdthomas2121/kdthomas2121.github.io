About KDTIVI

KDTIVI is a modern media player built for IPTV and Plex, emphasizing speed, simplicity, and reliability. Whether you're streaming live TV or browsing your Plex library, KDTIVI provides a clean, intuitive interface that gets out of your way.

The app supports M3U and Xtream Codes playlist formats, allowing you to easily organize and access your content. With multiple player options, catch-up support, and full EPG integration, KDTIVI delivers a premium viewing experience on both iPhone and Apple TV.
