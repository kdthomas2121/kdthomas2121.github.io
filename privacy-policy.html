Privacy Policy for Where’s Amy?

Effective Date: 22 June 2025

Where’s Amy? (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and store your personal information — especially your location — when you use the Where’s Amy? mobile app.

🔐 What We Collect

  • Location Data: We collect your precise location when you choose to share it with your group. This helps power live tracking, directional navigation, and ping responses.
  • User Info: We collect your display name and email address (from sign-in providers like Google) to help you identify yourself to your group.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymised app usage statistics to help improve performance and user experience.

🧭 How Your Location Is Used

  • Only shared with your group members, and only when you allow it
  • Never broadcasted publicly
  • Stored securely in our backend to enable location history, pings, and compass directions
  • Not used for advertising or sold to third parties

You can disable location sharing at any time via app settings.

🛡️ Data Security

We use Firebase to store and manage user data. Firebase applies industry-standard security practices to keep your data safe. Access to location data is restricted to authenticated users within your group.

🧍‍♀️ Your Rights

  • You can request to delete your data at any time by contacting us at moneynomadapp@gmail.com
  • You can opt out of location sharing from within the app
  • We do not knowingly collect data from users under the age of 13
