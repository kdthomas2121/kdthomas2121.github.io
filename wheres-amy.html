About Where's Amy?

Ever lost your friends at a festival and spent hours trying to explain where you are? Where's Amy? solves this problem with real-time location sharing designed specifically for crowded events like music festivals, concerts, and large gatherings.

Instead of endlessly texting "I'm by the big tree" or "near the food trucks," simply share your live location with friends. The built-in compass points you directly to them, and smart pings let you signal your location without draining your battery.
